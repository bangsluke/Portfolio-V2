## Description

Notion is an all‑in‑one workspace that lets you write documents, manage tasks, organise projects, and centralise knowledge in one place. It combines flexible pages, databases, and collaboration tools with built‑in AI features that help automate workflows, generate content, and provide intelligent insights.
