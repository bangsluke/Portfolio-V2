## Full Role Description

V801 Vehicle Integration role for the Interiors team (VHE2), supporting various engineering issues and reporting to management.

Developed numerous tracking documents to support the team and optimised ongoing workflows to enhance day to day operations. Helped with cross VHE metrics reporting in a developed [[Excel]] document supported with [[VBA]] scripts and with data architecture planning for a new multi-program [[Power BI]] report.

Supported teams in meeting [[CAD]] [[MCI]] completeness targets for UXV0 and then took on the role of tracking Recycling and Renewables content in ensuring suppliers met [[Ford]]'s 20% renewables target for UXV1.

Developed a solution for extracting folders and files from [[SharePoint]] using [[VBA]] to extract the data into [[CSV]] and then a [[Python]] script to tag the files into categories using fuzzy matching ready for a searchable [[Power BI]] report, allowing the combination of multiple sites into one searchable location.

## Key Achievement

The set up and maintenance of the VHE2 metrics [[Excel]], supporting the team with a [[VBA]] script for easy update ready for management reviews, saving the team set up time for daily reporting.
